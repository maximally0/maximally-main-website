### *A Standardized Hierarchy for Innovation Evaluation*

---

## **1. Introduction**

The Maximally Judge Tier Framework defines the hierarchy, responsibilities, and criteria for all judges participating in Maximally-affiliated innovation events, hackathons, and programs.

This structure ensures credibility, transparency, and consistency across all judging panels while maintaining pathways for professional growth within the ecosystem.

The framework establishes **five distinct tiers** of judges, reflecting their experience, expertise, and leadership in innovation evaluation.

---

## **2. Objectives of the Tier System**

1. **Standardization:**
    
    To establish a clear and consistent benchmark for judging quality across events.
    
2. **Credibility:**
    
    To ensure that participants are evaluated by qualified and proven experts.
    
3. **Progression:**
    
    To enable emerging mentors and professionals to grow into senior judging roles.
    
4. **Transparency:**
    
    To give organizers and participants clear visibility into a judge’s experience and authority.
    
5. **Recognition:**
    
    To honor individuals who contribute meaningfully to the innovation and builder ecosystem.
    

---

## **3. Overview of Tiers**

| Tier | Title | Description | Badge Color |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Level 1** | Starter Judge | Entry-level professionals or early mentors beginning their judging journey. | Green |
| **Level 2** | Verified Judge | Experienced professionals with proven domain expertise and prior judging or mentoring experience. | Blue |
| **Level 3** | Senior Judge | Established leaders with extensive professional or entrepreneurial backgrounds and consistent judging experience. | Purple |
| **Level 4** | Chief Judge | Highly credible industry or academic leaders who oversee judging panels and set quality standards. | Gold |
| **Level 5** | Legacy Judge | Distinguished figures recognized for exceptional contributions to innovation and mentorship over time. | Red |

---

## **4. Tier Descriptions and Criteria**

### **Level 1 — Starter Judge**

**Purpose:**

To onboard emerging professionals who demonstrate strong potential, builder experience, and enthusiasm to mentor.

**Typical Profile:**

- Early-career professionals, recent graduates, or startup builders.
- Individuals with hackathon or competition participation experience.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Assist senior judges during evaluations.
- Review early-stage or beginner-level project submissions.
- Learn evaluation frameworks and mentoring best practices.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- Demonstrated experience in building, designing, or researching projects.
- Strong motivation to mentor and contribute to the builder community.
- Basic understanding of innovation and product development.

**Advancement Requirements:**

- Successful participation in at least two Maximally events.
- Positive feedback from senior judges or organizers.

---

### **Level 2 — Verified Judge**

**Purpose:**

To engage established professionals and mentors capable of providing high-quality evaluation and guidance.

**Typical Profile:**

- Founders, engineers, researchers, designers, or educators with tangible achievements.
- Individuals with prior judging or mentoring experience.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Evaluate submissions in official judging rounds.
- Provide structured, actionable feedback to participants.
- Represent Maximally standards during events.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- At least 2–3 years of professional experience in a relevant field.
- Evidence of judging, mentoring, or leadership in prior events.
- Publicly verifiable professional presence (LinkedIn, website, portfolio).

**Advancement Requirements:**

- Judged 3 or more Maximally or partner events.
- Demonstrated consistency, reliability, and feedback quality.

---

### **Level 3 — Senior Judge**

**Purpose:**

To appoint experienced professionals and innovators capable of leading judging tracks and mentoring new judges.

**Typical Profile:**

- Mid to senior-level professionals with 7+ years of experience.
- Startup founders, senior engineers, or research leads.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Lead judging tracks and supervise Starter and Verified Judges.
- Define and refine evaluation rubrics for specific tracks.
- Mentor newer judges in assessment methodology and communication.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- Minimum 7 years of professional experience or equivalent impact.
- Prior involvement as a Verified Judge in at least 3 events.
- Proven mentorship or leadership experience.
- Recognized expertise in one or more domains (AI, design, systems, etc.).

**Advancement Requirements:**

- Nominated by existing Chief or Senior Judges.
- Consistent record of quality feedback and participant satisfaction.

---

### **Level 4 — Chief Judge**

**Purpose:**

To oversee and maintain the integrity, ethics, and quality of the judging process at the highest professional level.

**Typical Profile:**

- Senior leaders, professors, investors, or founders with global or institutional credibility.
- Individuals recognized for thought leadership and consistent innovation contributions.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Oversee entire event judging processes.
- Ensure fairness, consistency, and adherence to Maximally judging standards.
- Conduct judge training and orientation sessions.
- Represent Maximally in conferences, publications, and media appearances.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- 10+ years of experience or equivalent leadership impact.
- Multiple verified judging experiences across events and institutions.
- Endorsement from at least two Senior or Chief Judges.
- Proven ethical conduct and mentoring record.

**Advancement Requirements:**

- Direct nomination and review by Maximally’s core team.
- Demonstrated excellence in both judging and community contribution.

---

### **Level 5 — Legacy Judge**

**Purpose:**

To honor exceptional individuals whose long-term contributions have shaped the innovation and builder ecosystem.

**Typical Profile:**

- Founders, educators, or researchers who have led transformative work in technology, design, or education.
- Mentors who have consistently supported Maximally’s mission over multiple years.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Serve as mentors and advisors to the judging community.
- Guide the evolution of Maximally’s judging standards and frameworks.
- Participate in special ceremonies, panels, and legacy initiatives.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- Recognized global impact in technology, innovation, or education.
- Proven history of ethical leadership, mentorship, and ecosystem building.
- Selected by Maximally’s founding or executive committee.

**Privileges:**

- Lifetime recognition.
- Permanent listing under the “Legacy Judges” section of Maximally’s website.
- Access to exclusive events and strategic forums.

---

## **5. Advancement Pathway**

Judges may advance through tiers based on demonstrated performance, verified experience, and contributions to Maximally events.

**Advancement Factors:**

- Number of events judged.
- Quality of participant and organizer feedback.
- Mentorship impact and initiative.
- Peer recommendations.
- Demonstrated alignment with Maximally’s mission and ethics.

**Promotion Process:**

1. Internal review by the Maximally judging committee.
2. Verification of credentials and past event participation.
3. Optional nomination from higher-tier judges.
4. Formal confirmation and public tier update.

---

## **6. Governance and Review**

- The **Maximally Judging Committee** oversees verification, tier promotion, and conflict resolution.
- All judges are subject to periodic review to ensure continued compliance with Maximally’s standards.
- Ethical breaches, inactivity, or repeated participant complaints may result in demotion or suspension.
- Tier assignments are at the discretion of Maximally and may be adjusted based on evolving standards.

## **8. Closing Statement**

The **Maximally Judge Tier Framework** establishes a global benchmark for credibility and mentorship in the innovation ecosystem.

It creates an inclusive yet meritocratic structure — allowing emerging builders to grow into respected evaluators while ensuring that the most credible minds continue to set the standard.

Every Maximally Judge represents more than expertise.

They embody the shared belief that *innovation deserves to be judged by those who’ve built before.*
