## About Page (story + proof)

Everything that shows **who you are, what you’ve built, and why it matters**.

- **Hero / Overview**: tagline + mission.
- **Impact Stats**: participants, events, countries, revenue, judges.
- **People Preview**: 3–6 leaders, link to `/people`.
- **Partners**:
    - Sponsors (Masters’ Union, MakeX, etc.)
    - Collaboration orgs (YRI, Calyptus, etc.)
    - Schools/colleges you’ve worked with.
- **Press Mentions**:
    - Articles, interviews, shoutouts, podcasts.
    - Can be cards with logos + links.
- **Initiatives**:
    - Hackathons (linked to Events)
    - Platform (MaximallyHack)
    - Federation (MFHOP)
    - Studios (content vertical)
- **Judges / Mentors (preview)**: 3–5 notable names, link to full directory.
- **Timeline**: CodeQuest → Make-a-thon → AI Shipathon → GIHS hackathons.
- **Testimonials / Quotes**: from judges, partners, participants.
- **Culture / Task Force**: 2–3 lines on team DNA.
- **Future Vision**: where you’re headed.
- **Media Kit**: downloadable logos, colors, press kit.
- **Awards / Recognition** (if/when you have them).
- **Global Reach Map**: optional visual of countries/regions.

---

## Resources Page (content + community hub)

Everything someone can **use, read, or join**.

- **Blog**: all your posts.
- **Webinars**: upcoming + past recordings.
- **YouTube**: embedded videos + link to channel.
- **Community**: Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp (or one landing explaining all).
- **Initiatives**: quick-access links to hackathons, MFHOP, platform, studios.
- **Docs & Guides**:
    - Judge handbook
    - Participant handbook
    - Sponsor/partner deck
    - Press kit
    - FAQs
- **Newsletter / Updates**: signup + archive.
- **Research / Reports (optional)**: whitepapers, case studies (e.g., Project SYMBIONT).
- **Social Media Links**: Instagram, LinkedIn, X.

---

## Events Page (all programs)

Everything **you host/run**.

- Hackathons (AI Shipathon, Code Hypothesis, Protocol 404, CodeGen, Hacktober, Codepocalypse).
- Bootcamps (Startup Bootcamp).
- Make-a-thons (Startup Make-a-thon).
- Each event card: name, date, desc, link, status.

---

## People Page (directories)

Everyone involved.

- **Core**: team members, Task Force.
- **Judges**: large searchable directory.
- **Mentors / Advisors** (if you want to separate from judges).

---

## Optional Standalone Pages

- **Press**: all media mentions + downloadable media kit.
- **Docs**: full repository of resources (like Devfolio’s Docs).
- **Careers / Join Us**: apply to Task Force.
- **Federation (MFHOP)**: dedicated explainer + membership info.

---

👉 Rule of thumb:

- **About** = *story + proof*.
- **Resources** = *content + community*.
- **Events** = *all programs you run*.
- **People** = *everyone involved*.
