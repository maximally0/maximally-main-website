About Page (Deep, Modular Version)
1. Hero / What Maximally Is

Strong one-liner: “Maximally is a builder-first platform for hackathons, competitions, and creative systems.”

Sub-head: short mission (not fluffy).

CTAs: “See Events” / “Our Platform” / “Join Community”.

2. Impact Snapshot

Stats: participants, events, countries, partners, judges.

Add date stamp for credibility.

Can include milestone badges like ₹2L revenue or 1000+ participants in 2 months.

3. What We Do (Core Work)

Events: Hackathons, bootcamps, make-a-thons.

Platform: Building MaximallyHack (hosting + judge dashboards + team formation).

Federation: MFHOP (network of hackathon organizers).

Studios: Content/media vertical (podcasts, reels, blog).

Each with a card: description + link.

4. People Behind It

Short intro to the Task Force culture.

Highlight 3–6 leaders.

Button: “See Core Team” and “See Judges”.

5. Partners & Collabs

Logo wall (Masters’ Union, MakeX, YRI, Calyptus, etc.).

Note what each brought (sponsors, outreach, mentors).

6. Judges & Mentors

Preview 3–5 notable names (with roles).

Button → /people/judges.

7. Press & Mentions

Cards for outlets you’ve been in (or even “coming soon”).

Optional featured quote.

8. Story & Timeline

Chronology:

CodeQuest (biggest school hackathon)

Maximally launch

Startup Make-a-thon

AI Shipathon

Grand Indian Hackathon Season

Keeps narrative clear.

9. Testimonials

Quote cards from judges, partners, or participants.

Optional: video snippets.

10. Future Direction

A short roadmap (what’s next): platform launch, federation scaling, more events.

Could end with: “We’re just getting started.”

11. Media Kit & Resources

Download brand assets (logo, colors).

Link to /resources.

12. Culture / Join Us

A short note on the Maximally Task Force (high-agency, builder-first).

Button: “Apply to join” or “Volunteer”.

Awards / Recognition if you get them.
